Massaquerade

Game Jam • Mystery • Dec 29, 2025

You're a hitman invited to a masquerade ball. Your invitation had a single word on it: "Traitor". Navigate a masquerade ball, interact with guests, gather clues, identify your target, and remove the traitor. Made for Winter MelonJam 2025 under the theme "Mask".

How to Play

Controls

  • WASD / Arrow Keys - move
  • Z / LMB - interact
  • X / RMB - skip dialogue
  • Num Keys - shortcut to select dialogue options
  • M - shortcut to murder!

Goal

Go around the masquerade ball and interact with guests to gather clues. Clues may unlock new dialogue options with people you've already spoken to. Once you think you've identified your target, murder them and see if you were right. Good luck.
