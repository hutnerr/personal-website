About

Rat Haven Studios is a small indie game studio formed by a group of friends in 2025. We create games for fun in our free time and make things we think would be fun to play. We also like to share our development process and post devlogs about our projects. While we have a core team, we often collaborate with friends across different projects. We hope you enjoy our games.
