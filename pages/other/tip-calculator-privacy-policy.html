Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how the Tip Calculator application ("the App"), developed by Hunter Baker, handles user information.

Information Collection and Use

The Tip Calculator app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. The App does not require account creation or user login. It does not request or access permissions such as contacts, storage, SMS, call history, microphone, camera, or location.

All calculations are performed locally on the user's device.

Local Data Storage

The App may store user preferences locally on the device, such as tip percentage, currency selection, or rounding settings. This data is stored solely on the user's device and is never transmitted to external servers or third parties.

You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the App or clearing the App's data through your device settings.

Third-Party Services

The Tip Calculator app does not use third-party analytics services, advertising networks, or tracking technologies.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since we collect no personal information from any users, the App is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
