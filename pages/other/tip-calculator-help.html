getting started

Tip Calculator is a simple, ad-free Android app for quickly calculating tips and splitting bills. Open the app, type in the bill amount, and the tip is calculated immediately based on your saved settings - no extra taps needed.

The main screen has two buttons in the title bar: a person icon to open the bill-splitting menu, and a gear icon to open settings. Settings persist between sessions so the app is always ready with your preferred configuration.

features

  • Rounding System - choose from no rounding, dynamic, always up, or always down
  • Currency Support - USD, GBP, EUR, INR, PHP, THB, or none
  • Default Tip Percentage - set your preferred tip so it's always ready
  • Bill Splitting - split evenly among up to 99 people
  • Themes - Gruvbox, Dracula, and Mocha presets
  • Ad-free & Offline - works entirely without internet or ads
