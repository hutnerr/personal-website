Project Completed

It has been quite a long time, but I have finally completed my Tip Calculator. I started this last November and got it mostly completed, but just pushed it off for a very long time. I decided to focus on it again and get it done. And after a few bug fixes and polishing touches, it is finally complete and ready to be shown to the world!

I fully intend to publish this project to the Play Store, but I'm stuck waiting to be accepted at the time of writing this. If there was anything I would've done differently, it would be to start the process of getting accepted to the Play Store sooner. I had no idea it would take this long, and if I started sooner, I could've had it published by now.

Anyway, the app is now complete and I'm very happy with how it turned out. It has a clean and simple design, and it works perfectly. I designed it to be very easy to just pull out and use. I did that though specific customizable settings and interface design choices. It's also Ad-free and completely free to use, so I hope it can be a useful tool and save people some time and effort.
